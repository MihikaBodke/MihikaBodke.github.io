TSEC CodeStorm is a student's forum that serves as a Campus Chapter of CodeChef. It works under guidance of H.O.D of IT department- Dr. Madhuri Rao, Prof.Sanober Shaikh and Prof.Chetan Agarwal. It provides a proficient platform for coding and software. The goal is to expand and surge opportunities for aspiring coders through our events and workshops. The major event organised by the committee is hackathon. It is a gathering where programmers collaboratively code in an extreme manner over a duration of time.
